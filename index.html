Erasmus + project in Greece!

Training Course "Walking on Refugees Shoes" (WRS) will take place in Ioannina , Greece 15-21 July 2020. It will be the first time a tc for the topic of the refugges will be inplemented inside a structure for accompined minors refugees as ICSD operates that center hosting 38 young minors refugess well as we operate an Asylum information centre in the city of Ioannina. Thats for the title "Walking in Refugees Shoes" as participants won't only have a training where they will gain valuable knoweldge but they will have all workshops inside our structures with our experienced staff while they will see in first hand the mountain challenges refugees face for taking Asylum, Education, daily life and most of all a better future.
